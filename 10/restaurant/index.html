About us

Discover Our Restaurant Story

Every parent knows the heartache of slaving over a stovetop trying to prepare foods that are both nutritionally viable and able to tantilize the tastebuds of their picky eaters, only to be graced with dreaded criticisms about having the worst thing they've ever had to eat placed in front of them.

Vish's Grillicious Grill excels in the above regard and is about a stone's throw from transforming into a McDonald's that specializes only in chicken nuggets. It'll cost double for friends and family, too, because they should want to see us succeed and the children must pay for years of wasted effort in the kitchen that we'll never get back.
